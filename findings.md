## Top Consulting Website Design Patterns (Research)

### Sites Analyzed
Accenture, Deloitte, Bain, BCG, PwC, Prophet, frog, Thoughtworks, Goji Labs, Futurice, Instrument

### Universal Patterns (used by 6+ sites)
- Sticky/fixed navigation with mega-menu
- Full-width hero with video or dynamic content
- Card-based grid layouts for services/insights
- Dark/light mode or dark hero + light content sections
- Sans-serif typography for body, bold weights for headings
- Prominent CTAs integrated into content modules
- Trust signals: partnerships, awards, case studies
- Research/insights section demonstrating expertise
- Clean, spacious footer with structured links

### Key Design Takeaways
- Video heroes convey energy and modernity
- Card grids with hover effects create interactivity
- Color: bold accent + neutral base (dark + one signature color)
- Large typography creates confidence
- Whitespace = professionalism
- Social proof sections (logos, awards, testimonials) build trust
- Progressive disclosure through mega-menus
